All Features

Everything for
Secure Delivery

🏠
Permanent Home ID

Every US address receives a unique, permanent Home ID generated using one or more identification algorithms. The code is tied to the physical address — not the resident — and never changes.

👥
Resident Registry

Register all current residents at your address. Mail addressed to unregistered names is automatically returned to sender — protecting sensitive correspondence.

🛡️
Anti-Spoofing System

Multi-factor verification, device authentication, and behavioral pattern detection prevent any unauthorized use of your Home ID.

✅
Last-Mile Verification

At the final delivery step, the driver scans your door label and the system confirms it matches the package's destination Home ID.

🔐
Privacy-Preserving Shipping

Shipping labels display only your Home ID — never your name or address. Your personal information is never visible at any stage of the external shipment.

🏛️
Address Update System (AANS)

Moving? Use our checkbox interface to notify only the institutions you choose: IRS, banks, courts, clinics, employer, university, and more.

Our Story

About SaveCode

SaveCode was born from a simple frustration — receiving tax notices, bank statements, and court documents intended for strangers who once lived at the same address.

We built SaveCode to give every home a permanent, verifiable identity — a single code that connects the physical address to its current residents, protects incoming mail, and enables the next generation of delivery technology.

Our system is the first of its kind: combining address identification, resident verification, privacy-preserving shipping, and autonomous delivery compatibility into one unified platform.
