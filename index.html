Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SaveCode really free? +
Yes. Registering your home and generating your Home ID is completely free. Future premium features like metal plates and delivery integrations will be optional paid upgrades.
What if I move to a new address? +
Your Home ID is tied to the address, not to you. When you move, simply register your new address. You can then use the Address Update feature to notify your chosen institutions of your new Home ID.
How is SaveCode different from a ZIP code? +
ZIP codes identify areas covering thousands of addresses. Your SaveCode Home ID is unique to your specific address — even your specific unit or apartment.
Who can access my Home ID information? +
Only parties you explicitly authorize. The system uses a consent-based architecture — you control exactly who can access your information through our checkbox authorization interface.
When will Amazon and FedEx accept my Home ID? +
We are actively pursuing partnerships with major delivery companies. Our goal is to have delivery integration live by late 2026. Register today to be first in line when it launches.
