All Features

Everything for Secure Delivery

Nine powerful features working together to protect every delivery, every time.

🏠
Permanent Home ID

Every US address receives a unique, permanent Home ID generated using one or more identification algorithms. The code is tied to the physical address — not the resident — and never changes.

👥
Resident Registry

Register all current residents at your address. Mail addressed to unregistered names is automatically returned to sender — protecting sensitive correspondence like tax documents and bank statements.

🛡️
Anti-Spoofing System

Multi-factor verification, device authentication, and behavioral pattern detection prevent any unauthorized use of your Home ID. Attempts are blocked, logged, and you are alerted immediately.

✅
Last-Mile Verification

At the final delivery step, the driver scans your door label and the system confirms it matches the package's destination Home ID — preventing wrong-door deliveries even in apartment buildings.

🔐
Privacy-Preserving Shipping

Shipping labels display only your Home ID — never your name or address. Your personal information is never visible at any stage of the external shipment handling process.

🏛️
Address Update System (AANS)

Moving? Use our checkbox interface to notify only the institutions you choose: IRS, banks, courts, clinics, employer, university, immigration, insurance, and government agencies.

🤖
Autonomous Delivery

Compatible with aerial drones, ground robots, and self-driving vehicles. Your Home ID is the universal machine-readable delivery target for all current and future autonomous systems.

🔗
Open Integration API

Amazon, FedEx, UPS, USPS, and government agencies can integrate with SaveCode through our authenticated API with full access control and query validation.

The Process

How SaveCode Works

1

Register Your Address

Enter your US address into our secure registration system. Our algorithm generates a permanent, unique Home ID instantly. The code is tied to your physical address — not your name — and never changes.

2

Place Your Label

Print your official SaveCode label and place it on your front door and mailbox. The label displays your Home ID and an optional QR code for instant verification by delivery drivers and autonomous systems.

3

Register Your Residents

Add all current residents to your Home ID. Mail addressed to unregistered names is automatically returned to sender — protecting your household from sensitive mail intended for previous occupants.

4

Every Delivery Verified

At the moment of delivery, the driver or autonomous system scans your door label and confirms it matches the package's destination Home ID. No match — no delivery. Your package always reaches the right door.

Our Story

About SaveCode

SaveCode was born from a simple frustration — receiving tax notices, bank statements, and court documents intended for strangers who once lived at the same address. We realized this wasn't just an inconvenience. It was a systemic failure affecting millions of American homes every day.

We built SaveCode to give every home a permanent, verifiable identity — a single code that connects the physical address to its current residents, protects incoming mail, and enables the next generation of delivery technology.

Our system is the first of its kind: combining address identification, resident verification, privacy-preserving shipping, and autonomous delivery compatibility into one unified platform.
