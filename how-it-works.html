Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SaveCode really free? +
Yes, registering your Home ID is completely free. Always. SaveCode's mission is to give every American home a permanent identity, and we believe that should never cost anything.
What happens if I move? +
Your Home ID stays with the address — not with you. When you move, you register a new Home ID for your new address. The old address keeps its ID for the next residents.
Do carriers support SaveCode? +
We are actively working on carrier integrations. Our 2026 roadmap includes partnerships with major US carriers. Early adopters will be first to benefit when integrations go live.
Is my personal data safe? +
Absolutely. Your name and address are never shared with third parties. We use enterprise-grade encryption and your data is never sold. Privacy is at the core of everything SaveCode does.
Can I use SaveCode for a business address? +
Yes! SaveCode works for residential and commercial addresses. Businesses can register their address and manage employee mail through our enterprise dashboard.
