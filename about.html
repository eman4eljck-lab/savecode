The Problem We Solved

Why We Built SaveCode

SaveCode was born from a simple frustration — receiving tax notices, bank statements, and court documents intended for strangers who once lived at the same address. We realized this wasn't just an inconvenience. It was a systemic failure affecting millions of American homes every day.

The root cause? Addresses have no identity. A street address tells you where a place is — but nothing about who lives there, whether it's the right door, or if the mail is authorized to be delivered.

We built SaveCode to solve this problem once and for all. Every home deserves a permanent, verifiable identity — a single code that connects the physical address to its current residents, protects incoming mail, and enables the next generation of delivery technology.

"Every home in America deserves a permanent identity. Not just an address — an identity."
— Eman A. Eljack, Founder of SaveCode
